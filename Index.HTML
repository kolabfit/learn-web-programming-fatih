HTML(HyperText Markup Language)

HTML is a fundamental building block of the World Wide Web. It is a markup language used to structure the content and create the layout of web pages. HTML utilizes a system of tags and elements to define the structure and presentation of text, images, links, multimedia, and other components within a web document

In the digital realm HTML acts as the backbone that connects different elements together, enabling browsers to interpret and display content accurately. It provides a standardized format for organizing information, making it accessible and easily understood by both humans and machines.

how to make a spagheti :

    Ingredients :

  1. 300 g spaghetti
  2. 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  3. 50 g of finely chopped onion
  4. 3 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  5. 150 g of smoked beef, diced into 1 cm cubes
  6. 100 g button mushrooms, sliced
  7. 1 packet of Royco Chicken Soup
  8. 350 ml of water
  9. 150 ml of heavy cream
  10. 2 eggs, beaten
  11. 150 g grated parmesan cheese
  12. 1 teaspoon of powdered white pepper

Undered List

    How to make it :

  • Boil the spaghetti until it reaches an al dente texture or still feels chewy. Drain and set aside.
  • Mix together eggs, pepper, and parmesan cheese until well combined. Set aside.
  • Heat oil, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant. Add smoked beef and button mushrooms, sauté until wilted
  • Pour water and Royco Creamy Chicken Soup, cook while stirring until thickened. Add heavy cream, mix well
  • Add the spaghetti, stir. Add the egg and cheese mixture, stir quickly until well combined. Remove from heat.
  • Serve.
